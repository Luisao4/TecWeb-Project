  • Rules

    Nim is a combinatorial game,
    where two players alternately take turns in
    taking objects from several heaps.
    The only rule is that each player must
    take at least one object on their turn,
    but they may take more than one object in a single turn,
    as long as they all come from the same heap.
    The loser is the person who is forced to take the last object.
